Meet Donnie!
Me on Lunar New Year! 🏮
Hello all! My name is Don Dinh, but I am often called Donnie! I am an 8-year-old chocolate poodle 🍫. I was born in Korea, but my hoomans adopted me when I was four. My favorite foods are string cheese, watermelon and steak when my hoomans are nice! 🍉 🥩 I like long walks and sticking my head out of the car window. I dislike my cousin, Willow! 🐶 Be careful -- I am a trickster who will take full advantage of your hospitality!
